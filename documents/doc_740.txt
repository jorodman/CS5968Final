[1] President for the period 2013-2016 is José Antonio Martins Fernandes . [2] [1] [3] [4] History [ edit ] CBAt was founded on December 2, 1977. It replaced the Federação Brasileira de Sportes Atleticos, which was founded in 1914. [5] Former president until 2013 was Roberto Gesta de Melo .
