It was the 15th edition of the tournament was held from 13 July until 19 July 1992. Unseeded qualifier Andrei Medvedev won the singles title. Finals [ edit ] Singles [ edit ] Andrei Medvedev defeated Wayne Ferreira , 6–1, 6–4, 6–7 (5–7) , 2–6, 6–1 It was Medvedev's 2nd singles title of the year and of his career.
