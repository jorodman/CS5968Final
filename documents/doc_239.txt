[2] Music video [ edit ] A music video was released simultaneously with the single, directed by Malia James and with the participation of Dina Shihabi . The video finds Anthony Kiedis leading cops on a wild chase through backyards, house parties, in and out of apartments and past paparazzi after stealing items from a convenience store. Flea 's wife, Melody Ehsani , also makes an appearance in the video along with stunt coordinator Airon Armstrong (who played Michael Myers in Halloween Kills ) as one of the cops. [3] [4] [5] [6] Live performances [ edit ] The song made its live debut on April 1, 2022 on Jimmy Kimmel Live and has been played at nearly every show on the band's Global Stadium Tour .
