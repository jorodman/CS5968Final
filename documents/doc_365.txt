Playing career [ edit ] Grote played club football for FC Bremerhaven , VfB Oldenburg and Hamburger SV II . [1] Managerial career [ edit ] Grote was assistant manager at VfB Lübeck between 2004 and 2006, and managed various youth teams at SV Werder Bremen between 2008 and 2020.
