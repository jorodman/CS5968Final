Her work has been exhibited at the Rhode Island School of Design Museum ; Harvard Art Museums ; Guangzhou Biennial of Photography, China; Tang Museum, New York; and The Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History . Matthew is a professor of art (photography) in the University of Rhode Island 's Department of Art and Art History.
