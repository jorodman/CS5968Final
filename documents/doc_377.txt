Village in Ardabil, Iran Kinu كينو village Country Iran Province Ardabil County Germi Bakhsh Anguti Rural District Angut-e Gharbi Population (2006) • Total 129 Time zone UTC+3:30 ( IRST ) • Summer ( DST ) UTC+4:30 ( IRDT ) Kinu ( Persian : كينو , also Romanized as Kīnū ) is a village in Angut-e Gharbi Rural District , Anguti District , Germi County , Ardabil Province , Iran . At the 2006 census, its population was 129, in 24 families. [1] References [ edit ] Iran portal ^ "Census of the Islamic Republic of Iran, 1385 (2006)" (Excel) . Statistical Center of Iran .
