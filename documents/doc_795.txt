( November 2014 ) ( Learn how and when to remove this template message ) The Perfidy of Mary is a 1913 silent film directed by D. W. Griffith and starring Dorothy Gish and Mae Marsh as cousins Rose and Mary, with Walter Miller as Rose's suitor, Harry Hyde as Mary's suitor, and Lionel Barrymore as Mary's father. The picture has comedic overtones and also features Henry B. Walthall as a poet. Cast [ edit ] Dorothy Gish ... Rose Mae Marsh ... Mary Walter Miller ... Rose's Suitor Harry Hyde ...
