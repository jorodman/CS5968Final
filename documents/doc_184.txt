The town is easily reachable by train, the station of Verdello-Dalmine is in fact located on Verdellino's soil and the stop is one of several on the railroad line that connects the two cities of Bergamo and Milan. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 7,186 and an area of 3.8 square kilometres (1.5 sq mi). [3] Verdellino borders the following municipalities: Boltiere , Ciserano , Levate , Osio Sotto , Verdello .
