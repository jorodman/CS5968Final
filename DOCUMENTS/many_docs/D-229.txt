The team, also known as the DSL Pirates , plays in the Boca Chica , Dominican Republic , as a member of the Boca Chica North Division and are affiliated with the Pittsburgh Pirates . History [ edit ] Past players include Aramis Ramírez , José Guillén and Ronny Paulino . The club was known as the Dominican Summer League Pirates 1 , or DSL Pirates 1 , between 2012 and 2013, when the Venezuelan Summer Pirates were relocated to the Dominican Republic to become the Dominican Summer League Pirates 2. [1] In 2014, the Pirates returned to fielding one DSL team, and the team became known once again as the DSL Pirates.
