 Constituency of the National Assembly of Pakistan created in the 2018 delimitation exercise NA-38 Karak Constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan Region Karak District Electorate 404,458 Current constituency Member(s) Vacant Created from NA-15 Karak NA-38 Karak ( این اے-38، کرک ) is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan . It covers whole of district Karak . The constituency was formerly known as NA-15 Karak from 1977 to 2018. The name changed to NA-34 Karak after the delimitation in 2018 and to NA-38 Karak after the delimitation in 2022.
