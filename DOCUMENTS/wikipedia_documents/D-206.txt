 Q School – Event 1 Tournament information Dates 16 May 2022 – 21 May 2021 ( 2022-05-16 – 2021-05-21 ) Venue Ponds Forge International Sports Centre City Sheffield Country England Organisation World Snooker Tour Format Qualifying School Highest break Michael Georgiou (139) Qualifiers Rod Lawler Fergal O'Brien Andy Lee Bai Langning ← 2021 – Event 3 2022 – Event 2 → Snooker tournament Q School 2022 – Event 1 was the first of five qualifying tournaments for the 2022–23 snooker season . It took place from 16 May to 21 May 2022 at the Ponds Forge International Sports Centre in Sheffield , England . Format [ edit ] The event was played in a knockout system with the winner of each section earning a two-year tour card to play on the main tour for the 2022–23 and 2023–24 seasons. All matches were the best-of-seven frames.
