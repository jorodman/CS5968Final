There were a total number of 77 participating athletes, with ten qualifying heats and the final held on Sunday 25 August 1991. The world record , African record , and European record were broken by Carl Lewis , Frankie Fredericks , and Linford Christie , respectively. The then world record holder Leroy Burrell also bettered his previous mark of 9.90 seconds while Ray Stewart set the Jamaican record for the event.
