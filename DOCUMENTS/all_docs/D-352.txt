It forms part of the 2010–11 UCI America Tour . The tour was won by Marco Arriagada riding for the Chile national team. [1] The previous edition was won by Vincenzo Nibali , from Liquigas–Doimo , who came ahead of José Serpa , from Androni Giocattoli .
