[2] As the observatory's coordinator, he is responsible for close encounters computation of near-Earth objects (NEOs), [3] orbit computations, and identification of asteroids (about one thousand, included 17 NEOs). The Observatory's focus is the examination and tracking of NEOs in Solar System . [4] The Nysa asteroid 7866 Sicoli , discovered by Edward Bowell at Anderson Mesa Station in 1982, is named in his honor.
