The winner Hungary qualified for the eighth FIFA World Cup held in England . Matches [ edit ] 25 April 1965 Austria 1–1 East Germany Hof 46' Report Nöldner 74' Praterstadion , Vienna , Austria Attendance: 57,312 Referee: Mihailescu ( Romania ) 23 May 1965 East Germany 1–1 Hungary Vogel 17' Report Bene 28' Zentralstadion , Leipzig , East Germany Attendance: 98,765 Referee: Johansson ( Sweden ) 13 June 1965 Austria 0–1 Hungary Report Fenyvesi 44' Praterstadion , Vienna , Austria Attendance: 70,067 Referee: Schwinte ( France ) 5 September 1965 Hungary 3–0 Austria Farkas 4' Fenyvesi 35' Mészöly 70' ( pen. ) Report Népstadion , Budapest , Hungary Attendance: 71,950 Referee: Gorączniak ( Poland ) 9 October 1965 Hungary 3–2 East Germany Rákosi 41' Novák 53' Farkas 80' Report P. Ducke 31' , 71' Népstadion , Budapest , Hungary Attendance: 73,153 Referee: Andziulis ( Soviet Union ) 31 October 1965 East Germany 1–0 Austria Nöldner 1' Report Zentralstadion , Leipzig , East Germany Attendance: 86,584 Referee: Carswell ( Republic of Ireland ) Hungary qualified. This is the first time Austria failed to qualify. The previous absences of the Austrian team at World Cup were all due to withdrawals (1938, 1950, and 1962).
