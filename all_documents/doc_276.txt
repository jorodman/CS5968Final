[5] [6] [7] CBL proteins, such as CBLC, are phosphorylated upon activation of a variety of receptors that signal via protein tyrosine kinases. Through interactions with proteins containing SRC (MIM 190090) homology-2 (SH2) and SH3 domains, CBL proteins modulate downstream cell signaling (Keane et al., 1999).[supplied by OMIM] [7] Interactions [ edit ] CBLC has been shown to interact with FYN [8] and Epidermal growth factor receptor .
