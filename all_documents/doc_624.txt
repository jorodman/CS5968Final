The shadow cabinet was made up of members of the Liberal Party and the National Party of Australia in a Coalition agreement. Brogden led from the majority Coalition partner, the Liberal Party and served as leader of the opposition from 28 March 2002 to 1 September 2005. The minority Coalition partner, the National Party was led by Andrew Stoner MLA for the majority of this period.
